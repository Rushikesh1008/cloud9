Welcome to Cloud 9

Your tranquil Oasis in Murbad!

View Amenities View Gallery
Nestled in the heart of Murbad's breathtaking landscape, Cloud9 is just a short drive from Mumbai, Kalyan, and Thane. Our retreat offers the perfect blend of comfort, adventure, and natural beauty making it the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of the city life!

Imagine waking up to the gentle sounds of nature, surrounded by lush greenery and the promise of a day filled with relaxation and fun. Our eight custom designed bedrooms provide ample space for families, groups of friends, or corporate teams seeking a rejuvenating escape.

Your Ideal Getaway

Wake up to nature's tranquility, unwind in our inviting atmosphere, reconnect with loved ones and yourself.

  • Splash and Play: Take a refreshing dip in our 5-feet swimming pool, or groove to the rhythm in our rain dance setup!
  • Sunset Serenity: Ascend to our top terrace or view area to witness awe-inspiring sunsets that paint the valleys in hues of gold and orange.
  • Embrace Nature: Stroll through our sprawling gardens, where every corner reveals a new vista of vibrant flora and serene landscapes.
