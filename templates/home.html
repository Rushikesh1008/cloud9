Take a trip to Barvi Dam

Nestled amidst the lush greenery of the Western Ghats, Barvi Dam is a serene spot perfect for picnics and nature walks.

The dam, a major water source for Thane and Navi Mumbai, offers breathtaking views, especially during the monsoon when the surrounding landscape comes alive with cascading waterfalls and vibrant flora. Visitors can do bird-watching near the tranquil waters or simply relax by the banks, making it an ideal getaway for families and nature enthusiasts.

Experience Nature at Malshej Ghat

Malshej Ghat is a stunning mountain pass in the Western Ghats, renowned for its picturesque vistas, misty mountains, and rich biodiversity.

A paradise for trekkers, bird watchers, and photographers, Malshej Ghat is especially famous for its waterfalls and the pink flamingos that visit during the monsoon. The ghat offers numerous hiking trails and lookout points, providing panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and cliffs, making it a must-visit for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Explore Kondana Caves

Shivneri Fort, the birthplace of the legendary Maratha warrior King Shivaji, is a historical marvel perched atop a hill.

The fort offers a glimpse into the rich history and architectural brilliance of the Maratha era, with well-preserved structures such as temples, water systems, and a museum. A trek to Shivneri Fort not only provides an enriching historical experience but also rewards visitors with stunning views of the surrounding countryside, making it a significant attraction for history buffs and trekkers alike.

Tour the Magnificient Shivneri Fort

The ancient Kondana or Konkana Caves, located near Murbad, are a group of rock-cut Buddhist caves that date back to the 1st century BCE.

These caves, carved into a cliff face, feature intricate sculptures, stupas, and viharas, showcasing the exquisite craftsmanship of the period. A visit to Konkana Caves offers a serene and educational experience, transporting visitors back in time to explore the spiritual and artistic heritage of ancient India, making it a must-see for history enthusiasts and cultural explorers.

Trek at Naneghat

Naneghat Trek is a popular hiking trail that takes adventurers through the scenic landscapes of the Western Ghats to an ancient mountain pass.

The trek, suitable for beginners and experienced hikers alike, offers breathtaking views of lush valleys, dense forests, and majestic peaks. At the summit, trekkers can explore ancient rock-cut inscriptions and caves, remnants of the region's rich history. The trail is particularly enchanting during the monsoon season, with cascading waterfalls and verdant greenery, providing a perfect blend of nature and history
